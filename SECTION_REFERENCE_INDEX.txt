RECOMMENDED REVIEW ORDER

1. PF & ESI Registration — already in progress, finish this one first
   since it's the most-tested pattern (tabbed ledger, registry hero) and
   any fixes here may reveal issues common to other templates
2. PF Compliance — second largest category (18 services), dark/statutory
   identity, most structurally different from Registrations
3. Employment Agreements — also 18 services, different again
   (editorial/parchment), worth locking early given category size
4. Payroll Processing — 9 services, dashboard-card pattern
5. Flexi Staffing — 10 services, the most photography-dependent template
6. PF & ESI Returns Filing — only 1 more service inherits this template,
   lowest priority by volume, though still worth getting right since it's
   the most calm/spacious template and a good contrast reference

Once all 6 are locked, the patterns established in each become the basis
for building out the remaining ~85 individual service pages across all
six categories — each inheriting its category's now-approved template.

---

CATEGORY LANDING PAGES — separate scope

Per the locked plan, category landing pages (e.g. /registrations-licensing
itself, /labour-law-compliance itself) are a DIFFERENT page type from
these service-page templates — they are lean pages whose job is to list
and link to all services in that category, not to explain any one service
in depth. Category landing pages get their own section-reference files
once that work begins (after the 6 service templates above are locked).

The Registrations & Licensing category page already has a working build
(registrations_licensing_changes.txt — to be created once its redesign
per this principle is underway).
